# PawOps SEO Article Production Checklist

**Use this checklist for EVERY breed article before marking complete.**

---

## PRE-WRITING
- [ ] Target keyword researched (from 346-keyword master sheet)
- [ ] Search intent confirmed (informational vs commercial)
- [ ] Top 3 competing articles reviewed (what to beat)
- [ ] Unique angle identified (what makes ours different)

## WRITING
- [ ] 800-1,200 words (not too short, not filler)
- [ ] Target keyword in title, first paragraph, 1-2 H2s
- [ ] Conversational tone — contractions, varied sentence length, opinions
- [ ] At least 1 real data point or statistic per article
- [ ] Breed-specific details (not generic copy-paste across breeds)
- [ ] No "In conclusion" or "In this article we will discuss" (AI red flags)
- [ ] Include a surprising fact or counterintuitive insight per article
- [ ] PawOps voice: industry authority, not a single salon

## MEDIA
- [ ] 1 hero image (breed photo from gallery)
- [ ] 1-2 supporting images (grooming process, coat close-up, or infographic)
- [ ] Alt text on every image (breed name + descriptive)
- [ ] Image compressed for web (<100KB)

## SEO
- [ ] Meta title (50-60 chars, keyword + brand)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars, compelling + keyword)
- [ ] FAQPage schema (3-5 Q&As per article)
- [ ] Internal links: breed page, calculator, 1-2 related articles
- [ ] CTA: "Try PawOps Board Manager" with UTM link
- [ ] URL slug: /blog/[breed]-grooming-[topic]

## AI DETECTION AUDIT
- [ ] Run through AI detector (Originality.ai or GPTZero)
- [ ] Rewrite any flagged paragraphs
- [ ] Add personal voice markers ("Here's the thing...", "Most people don't realize...")
- [ ] Vary paragraph length (some 1 sentence, some 4)
- [ ] Include at least 1 imperfect/casual phrasing per article
- [ ] No bullet-point-heavy structure (mix with prose)
- [ ] Read aloud — if it sounds robotic, rewrite

## POST-PUBLISH
- [ ] Google Search Console indexing requested
- [ ] Shared to social channels
- [ ] Internal links added from existing breed page to new article
